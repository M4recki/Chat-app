# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Chat App community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

We encourage all participants to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Be collaborative** - Work together constructively to solve problems
- **Be direct but respectful** - Give honest feedback in a kind manner
- **Be open-minded** - Be receptive to different perspectives and ideas

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments or language (including racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia)
- Personal attacks or insulting language directed at others
- Spam or off-topic content
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any form of bullying or trolling

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the Chat App project, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Chat channels and forums
- Project events and meetings
- All project-related communications

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

### Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident (date, time, people involved, description)
2. **Report** to project maintainers:
   - Create a private issue (if available)
   - Email maintainers directly
   - Contact the GitHub organization
3. **Provide details** to help us understand and address the situation

We take all reports seriously and will handle them confidentially and fairly.

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning or request to stop the behavior
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Permanent removal from the project

The severity of consequences depends on the nature and frequency of violations.
